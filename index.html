Techmaniac

Made by: JohnGabrielUK

A Roy Webb, couch potato extraordinaire, was sitting amidst his pile of useless old tat one day, when suddenly a strange wind swept through the room and catapulted his beloved possessions into a strange land known only to him as "the outdoors"! Faced with the loss of the only source of joy in his life, Roy must venture forth and retrieve his hoard before someone else finds it and flogs it on eBay!

RetroArch

Made by: RetroArch & gadsby

RetroArch is a versatile frontend for emulators, game engines, and media players. It enables you to run classic games on a wide range of computers and consoles through its slick graphical interface. Settings are unified, so configuration is done once and for all. In addition to this, you are able to run original game discs (CDs) from RetroArch.
